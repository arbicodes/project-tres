What are header tags?

HTML header tags, commonly denoted as <h1>, <h2>, <h3>, etc., are used to define headings in a webpage.

They provide structure and hierarchy to the content, indicating the importance of different sections within a document. The number in the tag represents the level of importance, with <h1> being the most significant and <h6> the least.

What are font weights?

Font weight is a typographic property that determines the thickness or boldness of characters in a typeface.

It is expressed numerically, with common values ranging from 100 (ultra-light) to 900 (extra-bold), and it influences the visual prominence of text.
